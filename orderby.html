04 — DIGITAL KHATA

The notebook, upgraded to a real ledger

Udhaar is the original buy-now-pay-later — built on trust, run on paper. OrderBy makes it digital without losing the trust: culturally native, and the reason shops stay.

handshake

Two-way handshake

The shop requests udhaar; the customer accepts on their phone. No more "he said / she said" — both sides agree before it's booked.

account_balance

A real ledger

Every debit and repayment is recorded. Balances are derived, never guessed. The notebook becomes an auditable account both sides can trust.

verified

Settle anytime

Customers see their balance and settle monthly, just like always — except now nothing is lost, disputed, or forgotten.
