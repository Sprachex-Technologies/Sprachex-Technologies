Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account deletion take? expand_more

Your account is deactivated immediately. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days in compliance with privacy regulations. Backups may take up to 90 days to expire.

Can I recover my account after deletion? expand_more

No, account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data will be permanently removed from our systems. You can create a new account after deletion if needed.

What about my subscription or billing? expand_more

Any active subscriptions will be cancelled immediately. We will not charge you further. Depending on your payment terms, you may be eligible for a refund.

Is my data truly deleted? expand_more

Yes. We permanently delete all personal data within 30 days in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. Some data may be retained for legal requirements but will be anonymized and inaccessible.

Do you need email confirmation? expand_more

Yes. We send a confirmation email to verify the deletion request. You must click the confirmation link within 7 days to complete the process. This ensures the deletion request is legitimate.
